OLÚSÉGUN

Olusegun Anthony Michael, also known as OLÚSÉGUN born February 21st is an Afrocontemporary artiste, multi instrumentalist, singer, song writer, producer, music composer and energetic live band performer from Ondo State Nigeria. OLÚSÉGUN formed his band in 2021 known as Olusegun and The Stallion and they’re known for blending traditional African rhythms with modern elements to create a unique and captivating musical experience. Olúségun and The Stallion band has performed on several stages both as a guest artist and as a host; The New Afrika Shrine (Felabration), The Hook bar and Grill Lounge, Sixty by ChefLu, Bature Brewery.

They’re also known for their popular phrase “KUTUPA KUTUPA” and the audience responds with “Esin Gbangba”
