LIN NJOROGE

Lin Njoroge is a powerful Afrobeat and Afro-house singer/songwriter who hails from Nairobi, Kenya's vibrant and diversified musical scene. She delivers to the global music industry a unique blend of traditional Kenyan rhythms and contemporary sounds. She also has a notable proficiency in English, Swahili and Kikuyu languages. The rich cultural tapestry of Kenya is a major influence on Lin's musical journey; the rhythmic beats of Afrobeat and the customary melodies of Kikuyu Afrohouse serve as special sources of inspiration.
