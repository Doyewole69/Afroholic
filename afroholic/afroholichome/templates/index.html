DEPARTMENTS

PUBLISHING

This department has direct relationships with Performing Rights Organizations and other collection societies across the globe. With years of experience our team can pitch and collect on your behalf increasing growth and revenue.

DISTRIBUTION

Grow your audience and distribute your content globally. Our distribution platform allows the artist to monetize music videos, compositions, and sound recordings. WIth an easy to use interface you can view your analytics, handle splits, and manage royalties.

CONSULTING

With over 20 years in the music industry Immensum Music has shaped many artist’s careers from around the globe. Our consultation department focuses on marketing, artist development, and industry resources to help you reach your goals.

RIGHTS

Any time your song is broadcast on the radio or TV, or played live in concert, your “neighboring rights” are due. We manage complex ownership splits by territory for every asset type and set protection policies to meet your goals as a creator.

MANAGEMENT

Our management department consistently shares their experience, network, and knowledge across teams around the world giving each client an advantage above the rest.The team will implement strategy in all aspects of the client's career.
