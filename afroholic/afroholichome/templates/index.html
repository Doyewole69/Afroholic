ABOUT US

Why Choose Us?

At Afroholic Music, we offer all-round support to artists through expert sync and licensing services, global distribution, and strategic artist development. With over 20 years of combined industry experience, we help artists grow, earn, and thrive — from royalty splits to live performances.

Our team ensures your music reaches new audiences via films, TV, and commercials, while also delivering top-tier support for unforgettable live events. Every artist receives personalized management and marketing strategies designed for real impact. Choose Afroholic Music — where your talent meets opportunity, and your sound reaches the world.

Our Services

MUSIC SYNC AND LICENSING.

We elevate artists music career with our specialized sync and licensing services. We facilitate partnerships between artists and various media platforms, ensuring your music reaches new audiences through films, TV shows, commercials, and more. Let us help you navigate the licensing landscape and maximize your earning potential while showcasing your talent to the world.

DISTRIBUTION AND MANAGEMENT.

Through our distribution platform, we assist artists in sharing their music worldwide, with the primary goal of fostering growth. Additionally, we aid artists in generating income from every piece of work they release. Even in split sharing and royalties.
Our management team crafts customized and detailed strategies tailored to each artist, ensuring they can reach maximum and achievable goals

CONSULTING

With a team boasting over two decades of collective experience, this sector of the company focuses on artist development, strategic resource allocation, and effective music marketing. Our mission is to support artists in reaching their fullest potential and expanding their audience reach.

SHOWS & LIVE PERFORMANCE

We bring a difference in our live performances with our comprehensive range of services tailored to meet the unique needs of artists and event organizers. From stage setup and technical support to artist bookings and venue coordination, we're here to ensure your live shows are unforgettable experiences for both performers and audiences alike.
